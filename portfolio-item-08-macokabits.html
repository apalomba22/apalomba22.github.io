Maçokabits

iOS App

Experience at: Apple Developer Academy Date: 28/09/2022 Role: iOS Developer

A tool that helps gamers to have better habits, by tracking gaming session time and hinting them with useful advice on healthy lifestyles.

Introduction

This project focuses on creating an application that tracks gaming session time and provides users with helpful advice on maintaining their physical and mental well-being. By harnessing the power of technology, we aim to empower gamers to make informed decisions about their gaming habits and foster a positive gaming experience.

My role

As an iOS Developer, I invested my time in crafting the user interface (UI) by coding the view elements, ensuring a visually appealing and user-friendly experience. I also developed the ViewModels and Models of the app, which served as the backbone of its functionality.

Furthermore, I utilized Source Control through GitHub to manage version control, ensuring a seamless collaboration process within the development team. By actively using Source Control, we were able to maintain a structured and organized codebase, facilitating efficient teamwork and enabling effective tracking of project changes.
