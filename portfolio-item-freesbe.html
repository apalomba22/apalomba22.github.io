Freesbe

iOS App

Experience at: Apple Developer Academy Date: 01/12/2022 Role: iOS Developer

The app provides a way for Academy Students who want to organise an activity by displaying it in community feed, looking for help and participants.

Introduction

An app designed exclusively for Academy Students, providing them with a platform to organize and participate in various activities. This app serves as a dynamic community feed, where students can share their activities, seek assistance, and find like-minded participants. By fostering a sense of community and collaboration, it empowers Academy Students to engage in meaningful and enjoyable activities during their learning journey.

My role

As an iOS developer on this project, I played a pivotal role in bringing the app to life. From creating elements of the user interface to managing the flow of data through the views, my contributions were instrumental in shaping the app's functionality and user experience.

Designing elements of the user interface, I aimed to ensure a visually appealing and intuitive app. By leveraging my skills in UI design, I crafted interfaces that not only showcased the activities in an organized manner but also provided an engaging and seamless user experience. Animations and transitions between views were meticulously implemented, adding a touch of dynamism and enhancing the overall user interface.

Additionally, I actively utilized Source Control through GitHub remote repository, ensuring efficient collaboration with my team members. By effectively managing the codebase and leveraging version control, I contributed to a smooth and streamlined development process.
